Privacy Policy for SnapCal

Last Updated: January 28, 2026 | Effective Date: January 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SnapCal ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use our mobile application.

By using SnapCal, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

Data Category Data Types Linked to You Purpose
Contact Info Name, Email Address Yes Account creation, authentication
Health & Fitness Height, Weight, Age, Gender, Activity Level Yes Calculate personalized calorie goals
User Content Photos of food Yes AI nutritional analysis, meal history
Identifiers User ID Yes Account management
Identifiers Device ID No Analytics, app functionality
Diagnostics Crash Data No App stability and bug fixes

3. Data NOT Used for Tracking

We do NOT use any collected data to track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • App Functionality: Provide AI-powered food analysis, calculate personalized nutrition goals, track your daily intake
  • Account Management: Create and manage your account via Google or Apple Sign-In
  • Subscription Management: Process payments and provide access to premium features
  • App Improvement: Fix bugs and improve app performance using crash data

5. Third-Party Services

We share data with the following service providers:

Provider Purpose Data Shared
Google Gemini AI Food image analysis Food photos (processed, not permanently stored by Google)
Firebase (Google) Authentication, database, storage Account info, health data, meal data, photos
RevenueCat Subscription management User ID, purchase status
Apple App Store Subscription billing Payment info (handled entirely by Apple)

6. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Browsing history
  • Financial information (payments handled by Apple)
  • Sensitive information beyond health metrics for calorie calculation

7. Data We Do NOT Sell

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties. Ever.

8. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored securely on Firebase servers (Google Cloud Platform)
  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Authentication uses secure OAuth 2.0 protocols
  • We implement industry-standard security measures

9. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Meal History & Photos: Retained while your account is active
  • Crash Data: Retained for up to 90 days

You may request deletion of all your data at any time.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information via the App
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Uninstall the App to stop all data collection

California Residents (CCPA)

You have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights.

European Residents (GDPR)

You have rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection to processing.

11. Children's Privacy

SnapCal is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
