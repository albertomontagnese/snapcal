Privacy Policy for SnapCal

Last Updated: January 28, 2026 | Effective Date: January 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SnapCal ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by SnapCal when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using SnapCal, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

Account Information:

  • Name (from Google/Apple Sign-In)
  • Email address (from Google/Apple Sign-In)

Health & Fitness Data:

  • Height
  • Weight
  • Age
  • Gender
  • Activity level
  • Weight goals

This information is used solely to calculate your personalized daily calorie and macronutrient goals.

User Content:

  • Photos of food you take within the App
  • Meal logs and nutritional data

B. Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data:

  • App usage statistics
  • Feature interactions
  • Session duration

Device Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers

C. Information from Third-Party Services

When you sign in using Google or Apple:

  • Basic profile information (name, email)
  • Authentication tokens

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide the Service: Analyze food photos using AI, calculate personalized nutrition goals, and track your daily intake
  • Improve the App: Understand usage patterns to enhance features and user experience
  • Manage Subscriptions: Process payments and provide access to premium features through RevenueCat
  • Communicate: Send important service announcements (if necessary)
  • Comply with Legal Obligations: Meet applicable legal requirements

4. How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information.

We share data only with the following service providers:

Provider Purpose Data Shared
Google Gemini AI Food image analysis Food photos (processed, not stored)
Firebase (Google) Authentication, database, storage Account info, meal data
RevenueCat Subscription management User ID, purchase status
Apple App Store Subscription billing Payment info (handled by Apple)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely on Firebase servers (Google Cloud Platform)
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Authentication is handled through secure OAuth 2.0 protocols
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures
Important: No method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Meal History: Retained while your account is active
  • Food Photos: Processed by AI and stored with meal records

You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 9).

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information via the App
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-Out: Uninstall the App to stop data collection

California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights including:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

European Residents (GDPR)

European residents have additional rights including:

  • Right to access and portability
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing

8. Children's Privacy

SnapCal is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.
